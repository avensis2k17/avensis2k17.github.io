Event Description

It’s the time for your bot to follow the line! Love coding and intrigued by robots? So, now is your chance to apply your algorithms to real life robots and see your code in action. IMAGINE-SENSE -DISCOVER ! LFR is an event in which an autonomous robot is made which is capable of following a line. This line can be a white line on a black background or a black line on a white background. The robot will cover the track following this line. The autonomous robot will begin its run from a predefined position in a dark isolated room. It will follow the line strip beneath it and would cover the track. The robot has to decide its path by sensing the line. The objective of an line follower is to find its path through the predefined end point in least possible time with accuracy. It’s the time for your bot to follow the line! Love coding and intrigued by robots? So, now is your chance to apply your algorithms to real life robots and see your code in action. IMAGINE-SENSE -DISCOVER !

Event Description

Robo Rugby is an event in which robots try to score a point by hitting the balls into the scoring zone at both end point of a playing table. A match of Robo Rugby involves two small robots and some balls of different colors. Every colored ball clutches different points. One robot is assigned to each team and the position of each ball at the end of the game governs the final score and hence the winner. It is the time to concentrate on the hacking and slashing of the robots. So, make your bots ready and leave them at the arena to feel the game. The game of SOCCER!!
