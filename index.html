About Us

Avensis, an unmatchable artefact of Maharaja Surajmal Institute of Technology is a blend of ingenuity, genius and creativity. Healthiest competition occurs when average people win by putting above average effort.This spirit of healthy competition is what AVENSIS seeks to fill its participants with. And what other way than two fun filled exciting days with a variety of events that test the students in a diversity of backgrounds .The two day extravaganza is a panorama of high octane energy and zeal. We endeavour to deliver surpassing emanation every epoch.
